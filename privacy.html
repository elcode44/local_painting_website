Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Artist Keith Lutz Painting ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website or contact us for services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our site or contact us:

  • Contact information you provide, such as your name, phone number, email address, and project details when you submit our contact form or email us directly.
  • Usage information if you accept analytics cookies, such as pages visited, browser type, and general location (city/region level) collected through Google Analytics.
  • Technical information automatically sent by your browser, such as IP address and device type, which may be processed by our hosting provider or analytics service.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to estimate requests and questions about our services
  • Schedule and perform painting and related work you request
  • Improve our website and understand how visitors use it (only if you accept analytics cookies)
  • Protect our website from abuse and spam

We do not sell your personal information.

Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses optional analytics cookies through Google Analytics to help us understand site traffic. These cookies are only loaded if you click "Accept Analytics" in our cookie banner. You can change your choice at any time using "Cookie Settings" in the footer.

Learn more about Google’s practices at Google Privacy Policy.

Contact Form Processing

If you submit our contact form, your message may be processed by Formspree (a third-party form delivery service) so we can receive your inquiry by email. Formspree handles submissions on our behalf and is used only to deliver messages to us.

How We Share Information

We do not sell or rent personal information. We may share information only with:

  • Service providers that help us operate the website (hosting, form delivery, analytics)
  • Authorities if required by law or to protect our legal rights

Data Retention

We keep contact inquiries only as long as needed to respond, provide services, and maintain business records. Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics settings.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect our website, including HTTPS encryption (when enabled by your hosting provider), spam protection on our contact form, and security headers. No website can guarantee complete security, but we take appropriate steps to reduce risk.

Your Choices & Rights

You may:

  • Decline analytics cookies using our cookie banner or footer settings
  • Contact us directly by phone or email instead of using the form
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you

California residents may have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected and to request deletion, subject to certain exceptions. We do not sell personal information.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are directed to adults seeking home improvement work. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
